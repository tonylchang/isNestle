Privacy Policy

THE SHORT VERSION

isNestle is built to collect nothing. There is no account, no analytics, no tracking, and no server run by us. By default the app works entirely offline and nothing about what you scan ever leaves your phone.

What we collect

Nothing. We operate no servers and store none of your data. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Camera

The camera is used solely to detect a barcode on your device, in real time. No photos or video are recorded, saved, or transmitted.

Scanning is offline by default

The app ships with a bundled product/ownership database, so scans are answered on your device. With the optional online lookup turned off (the default), no information about a scan leaves your phone.

Dataset updates

To keep its data current, the app may download an updated database file from a public, static host (GitHub Releases). This is a plain file download — it reveals nothing about what you have scanned or searched.

Optional online lookup (off by default)

You can turn on "Online lookup" to identify barcodes that aren't in the offline database. When (and only when) it is enabled, a scanned barcode that the app can't resolve locally is sent to Open Food Facts to look up the product. Only the barcode (and, inherent to any internet request, your device's IP address) is sent. We do not log or store it. Open Food Facts' own privacy policy applies to that request. You can turn the feature off at any time.

Stored on your device

Only your preferences (selected theme, online-lookup toggle) are saved locally on your device. They are never transmitted.

Third parties

Open Food Facts
Only when you enable online lookup, as described above.
Apple
Distribution happens through the App Store / TestFlight; Apple's standard policies apply to the download itself.

Children

isNestle does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change and the revised policy will be posted at this URL.
