WHY IT EXISTS

Some brands hide who owns them.

Nestlé is a vast conglomerate, and many of its brands are marketed without any visible link to the parent company. If you're choosing where your money goes, that's hard to see at the shelf. isNestle makes it one scan.

HOW IT WORKS

Three steps, on your device.

01

Scan

Point the camera at a barcode. Detection happens on-device — no photo is stored or sent.

02

Resolve

A bundled database maps barcode → brand → parent company, instantly and offline.

03

Decide

A clear verdict with the ownership chain — read it in a glance or study the full record.
